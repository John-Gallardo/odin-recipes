Squid Ink Pasta from Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

Image from iStockphoto

Description

A pasta dish with squid that's dyed black with the ink sac.

This recipe is from Golden Gully.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole squid
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Chili flakes
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Seafood stock
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Remove tentacles from squid & save the ink sac. Slice the squid up into strips.
  2. On a pan on low heat, add olive oil, chili flakes, garlic, and black pepper. Mix for a few minutes.
  3. Add squid strips & sautee for about 5 minutes on medium heat. Add the ink sac & mix well then top off with seafood stock. Cover on medium high heat for about 10 minutes.
  4. As that cooks, cook your pasta in salted water, and once finished cooking add that into your squid with some pasta water and parsley. Mix well and enjoy!
