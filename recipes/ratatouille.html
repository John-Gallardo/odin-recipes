Ratatouille

Image from Unsplash

Description

A French dish of stewed vegetables, made up primarily of eggplant, zucchini, and summer squash.

This particular recipe is from allrecipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt & ground black pepper
  • 1 small eggplant
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 yellow squash
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 3 tablespoons mascarpone cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Spread tomato paste onto the bottom of a 10-inch baking dish, & sprinkle with onion & garlic. Stir in water and 1 tablespon olive oil until combined then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange alternating slices of eggplant, zucchini, yellow squash, red bell peper, and yellow bellpepper, starting at the outer edge of the dish and working in a circle towards the center. Overlap some slices to display colors.
  4. Drizzle the remaining 3 tablespoons of olive oil; season with salt and peppe. Sprinkle with thyme leaves. Cover vegetables with a piece of parchment paper cut to find inside.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until vegetables are roasted and tender (approximately 45 minutes). Serve with mascarpone cheese if desired.
