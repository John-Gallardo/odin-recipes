Jake's Sandwich from "Adventure Time"

Image from Unsplash

Description

The greatest sandwich ever, made with ingredients such as a lobster soul, bacon, tomatoes, dill, diced boiled eggs, and etc.

This particular recipe is from fiction food cafe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Sub Roll
  • Cream Cheese
  • Pickles sliced length-wise
  • Fresh Dill
  • 2 Hardboiled Eggs
  • 1 Boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1-2 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Small sweet yellow onion
  • Salt
  • 3-5 Pieces of bacon, already cooked
  • Lobster Aioli
  • ~1/3 Cup Cooked Lobster
  • 2 Tbsp. Mayonnaise
  • 1 Tsp. Lemon juice
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • Fresh Dill (optional)
  • Sous-Vide
  • 1 Long, thin steak
  • Fresh Rosemary
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Moderately sprinkle both sides of steak with salt & then wrap with a few paper towels. Let it sit for ~30 minutes so the towels can soak up some of the juices
  2. Lobster Aioli (AKA Lobster Soul): Put all of the ingredients in a food processor or blender & pulse until fully combined. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  3. Slice the sub roll in half lengthwise & toast.
  4. Slice chicken breast in half lengthwise so you have 2 thin pieces. Season with salt & pepper, and cook using your preferred method: sautee, bake or grill. Set aside once done.
  5. Unwrap the steak & place it in a large freezer bag. Put a sprig of rosemary & thyme, & seal the bag, then sous-vide at 135 fahrenheit. Once done, place the steak with the chicken until ready to use.
  6. Lay your sub rolls toasted side up & spread cream cheese. Put dill on the half that will be the top. In this order, on the bottom half, lay 3 pickle slices, diced boiled eggs, chicken breast pieces, cucumber slices, tomato slices, onion & some salt, steak, bacon pieces, and lastly, the aioli on top of the cream cheese. Close the sandwich & enjoy!
